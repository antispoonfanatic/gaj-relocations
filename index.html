About Us

GAJ Relocation Services was established in the year 2021 with a strong foundation of many years of quality experience and expertise in the field. The name has its derivation from the initial letters of the names of its family members.

Our Mission

To provide customers a comprehensive solution for a safe and secure relocation of all legal goods, to offer quality and consistent services to clients of all sizes both domestically and internationally at reasonable rates, to ensure 100% customer satisfaction at all times.

Our Vision

To become the one-step solution for all relocation needs globally

Our Values

The core values of GAJ is GAJ:

  • Generous with our expertise
  • Accountable to our customers
  • Justice to our job
